###### A.1.9.1.2.3.1 - Additional Emergency Response Team Members [Core]

Soter Labs is permitted to assign more than two (2) of its team members to the permissioned Emergency Response Communication Channels defined in [A.1.9.1.3 - Emergency Response Communication Channels](18418289-ada8-401d-a992-7e38b235c866), as well as any Emergency Response calls.
